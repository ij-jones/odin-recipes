Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This spaghetti meat sauce recipe calls for simple ingredients from your kitchen to make the most flavorful meat sauce to serve over your pasta. This spaghetti sauce with ground beef is quick and easy to make yet full of comforting flavors, it will instantly become a family dinner staple.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cubes beef bouillion
  • 1 (8-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 2 teaspoons sugar white or brown
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • dash of garlic
  • ▢16 ounces spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet. (drain excess fat)
  2. Once cooked, throw in salt, pepper, tomato sauce and paste, water (with the bouillon cubes in it), sugar, basil, oregano and garlic. Simmer on low for an hour.
  3. A few minutes before the hour is done, cook box of spaghetti noodles as directed on package.
  4. Once the noodles are cooked, drain and add to spaghetti sauce.
